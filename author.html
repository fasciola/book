About the Author

A scholar, writer, and journalist from Sudan

Ahmad Attijani Ahmad Albadawi

Scholar · Researcher · Journalist

Address: Khartoum, Sudan

Occupation: Imam of Arrahad Mosque; Journalist with Sudanile newspaper, Assahafa newspaper, and many academic websites.

Ahmad Attijani Ahmad Albadawi is a distinguished scholar, researcher, and veteran journalist based in Khartoum, Sudan. For more than three decades, he has written widely on religion, philosophy, society, and culture — guided by the absolute conviction that Islam, properly understood, is an infinite message of mercy, wisdom, and peaceful coexistence, rather than extremism or hostility.

His mission is clean and direct: to present the original, universal, and merciful guidelines of Islam to a world that has too often seen only distorted depictions of it. He writes not merely for academic theological audiences, but directly for every single searcher of truth who has ever pondered on our human origins and destiny.

He considers this specific work — Spare Some Minutes of Your Time — to be his most crucial contribution because it was written not as an academic lecture, but as a personal letter directly addressed to you, the reader, inviting shared reflection.

He is the writer of numerous printed and forthcoming publications. He maintains an active presence on mainstream networks and is dedicated to inviting people to the peaceful invitation of Islam "in a way that is better."
